Interests

I love reading books. A lot. I love escaping reality (in short, healthy spurts) in favour of astounding, make-belief worlds craftily constructed by clever worded authors. Among my favourite authors are Agatha Christie, Sir Arthur Conan Doyle, Dan Brown, George R. R. Martin, J. K. Rowling, J. R. R. Tolkien, P. G. Wodehouse, Roald Dahl, and Sidney Sheldon.

I also love listening to music. It helps bring me up when I'm a little down, or bring me down when I'm a bit too up. You can check if we have similar taste in music by viewing my playlist here.

When I'm not reading or listening to music, I love to watch a good movie. You can see my list of favorite movies here.

I occasionally take to writing as well, but I can assure you, I don't write very well. Nonetheless, I enjoy praise, deserved or undeserved, sycophantic or otherwise and so, I will share my writings with you. Since I'm too lazy to start a blog, I just post them on facebook.
